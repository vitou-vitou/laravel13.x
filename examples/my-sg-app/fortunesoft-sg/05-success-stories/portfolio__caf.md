 CAF

 The client required a Internet Radio and Video content streaming mobile application

 
 

 
 Technology Used

 
 Android 
 Objective C 
 PHP 
 MYSQL 
 Firebase 
 Google APIs 
 
 
 

 
 
 Our Solution

 Our solution for the client requirement involved creation of native applications for Android and iOS to make the most out of the inbuilt features of the Android & iOS operating system and hardware. The application lets users explore and listen to internet radio stations and watch videos. The backend application of the application is built using PHP to enable the company to manage the radio stations available in the application, manage users, and manage video stations
