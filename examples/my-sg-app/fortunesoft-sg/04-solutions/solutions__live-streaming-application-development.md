 1. What are the key features of a live streaming application? 

 Key features include real-time video broadcasting, chat tools, adaptive video quality, content moderation, and analytics. These features ensure an engaging and smooth streaming experience for both viewers and creators. 2. How secure is a live streaming platform? 

 A live streaming platform is secure when using encrypted data transmission, secure login protocols, access control, and real-time monitoring to ensure privacy and safety for users. 3. Can the live streaming app be customized to match our business needs? 

 Yes, Fortunesoft develops customized live streaming apps to align with your business goals. Customization includes branding, functionality, and integration with third-party services to enhance the overall user experience. 4. What technologies do you use for live streaming app development? 

 Technologies like WebRTC, Wowza Streaming Engine, and RTMP (Real-Time Messaging Protocol) are employed to ensure low-latency, high-quality video streaming for a seamless user experience. 5. How do you ensure high-quality video streaming with minimal latency?
