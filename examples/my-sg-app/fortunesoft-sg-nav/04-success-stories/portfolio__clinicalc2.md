ClinicalC2 is the world’s first vendor scheduling platform specially designed for healthcare providers. They wanted a robust and easy-to-use scheduling system for their targeted users to organize and communicate with all vendors based on healthcare. Therefore, users can manage and coordinate with healthcare companies and hospitals quickly.

#### Technology Used

- React
- NodeJs
- MongoDB

## Our Solution

Recognizing the needs of the users and the requirements of the client, we used the AGILE methodology to build the vendor scheduling platform with elite functionalities like vendor handling, automated scheduling, advanced analytics, and more. As a result, we delivered a cutting-edge healthcare scheduling management SaaS platform that helped users to schedule and manage tasks without hassle.
