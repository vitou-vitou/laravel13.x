## Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We are here to help.

[1\. What is a medicine delivery app, and how does it work?](https://www.fortunesoftit.com/sg/solutions/medicine-delivery-app-development/#faq-collapse-0)

A medicine delivery app is a mobile platform that allows users to order prescription and over-the-counter medicines from nearby pharmacies. Users can upload prescriptions, make secure payments, and have medications delivered directly to their doorsteps, streamlining the pharmaceutical ordering process.

[2\. Is the app compliant with healthcare regulations?](https://www.fortunesoftit.com/sg/solutions/medicine-delivery-app-development/#faq-collapse-1)

Yes, the medicine delivery app is fully compliant with international healthcare regulations such as HIPAA. It ensures secure user access, encryption, and audit trails. In Singapore, it also complies with local guidelines such as PDPA and Health Sciences Authority (HSA) regulations to ensure data privacy and prescription validation.

[3\. Can customers upload prescriptions within the app?](https://www.fortunesoftit.com/sg/solutions/medicine-delivery-app-development/#faq-collapse-2)

Yes, customers can securely upload prescriptions via built-in image capture or file upload features. The app ensures that pharmacists can easily verify and dispense medications according to regulatory requirements in Singapore.

[4\. How does the AI-powered system enhance the app’s functionality?](https://www.fortunesoftit.com/sg/solutions/medicine-delivery-app-development/#faq-collapse-3)

The AI-powered system enhances the app by enabling features like smart prescription scanning, personalized medicine recommendations, demand forecasting, and order processing automation. In Singapore, this improves operational efficiency and provides users with a smoother experience.

[5\. How long does it take to develop a custom medicine delivery app?](https://www.fortunesoftit.com/sg/solutions/medicine-delivery-app-development/#faq-collapse-4)

Developing a custom medicine delivery app typically takes 3 to 6 months, depending on feature complexity, integrations, and compliance with local regulations in Singapore. The timeline may vary based on testing phases and approval processes.
