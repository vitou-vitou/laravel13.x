## Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We are here to help.

[1\. What are the key features of an online furniture store app?](https://www.fortunesoftit.com/sg/solutions/online-furniture-store-app-development-company/#faq-collapse-0)

Key features include a product catalog, AR-based room visualization, secure payment options, customer reviews, real-time order tracking, and easy returns. These features help users shop with ease and provide a seamless shopping experience.

[2\. How can a custom furniture shop mobile app enhance user experience?](https://www.fortunesoftit.com/sg/solutions/online-furniture-store-app-development-company/#faq-collapse-1)

A custom furniture app offers personalized recommendations, virtual try-on via AR, smooth navigation, and simplified checkout, enhancing user convenience and satisfaction.

[3\. Can the app support multiple payment options and integrate with existing systems?](https://www.fortunesoftit.com/sg/solutions/online-furniture-store-app-development-company/#faq-collapse-2)

Yes, the app supports multiple payment methods such as credit cards, digital wallets, and net banking. It integrates with existing systems like inventory, CRM, and ERP for a streamlined operational flow.

[4\. How do you ensure app security and data protection?](https://www.fortunesoftit.com/sg/solutions/online-furniture-store-app-development-company/#faq-collapse-3)

App security is ensured with encryption, secure APIs, regular security audits, and adherence to data protection regulations. These measures protect user data and prevent unauthorized access.

[5\. What is the typical timeline for developing a furniture shop mobile application?](https://www.fortunesoftit.com/sg/solutions/online-furniture-store-app-development-company/#faq-collapse-4)

Typically, the development of a custom furniture shops mobile app takes 2 to 4 months, depending on the features, design complexity, third-party integrations, and testing requirements.
