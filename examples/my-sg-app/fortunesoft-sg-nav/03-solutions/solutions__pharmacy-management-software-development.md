## Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We are here to help.

[1\. What is pharmacy management software, and how does it benefit pharmacies?](https://www.fortunesoftit.com/sg/solutions/pharmacy-management-software-development/#faq-collapse-0)

Pharmacy management software is a digital solution that automates tasks like prescriptions, billing, and inventory. It helps pharmacies improve accuracy, boost efficiency, and ensure regulatory compliance.

[2\. Can the software handle e-prescriptions and integrate with healthcare providers?](https://www.fortunesoftit.com/sg/solutions/pharmacy-management-software-development/#faq-collapse-1)

Yes, the software supports e-prescriptions and integrates seamlessly with healthcare providers and hospital systems. This allows pharmacies in Singapore to process prescriptions faster, reduce errors, and improve patient care coordination.

[3\. How does pharmacy management software improve customer service and medication adherence?](https://www.fortunesoftit.com/sg/solutions/pharmacy-management-software-development/#faq-collapse-2)

Pharmacy management software improves customer service by enabling quick prescription processing, real-time inventory updates, and personalized notifications. In Singapore, it also supports medication reminders and refill alerts to boost patient adherence and health outcomes.

[4\. How does the software ensure data security and compliance with healthcare regulations?](https://www.fortunesoftit.com/sg/solutions/pharmacy-management-software-development/#faq-collapse-3)

The software ensures data security through encryption, secure login protocols, and regular system audits. It also complies with Singapore’s healthcare regulations, including PDPA, to safeguard patient data and maintain trust.

[5\. What is the cost of developing a custom pharmacy management solution?](https://www.fortunesoftit.com/sg/solutions/pharmacy-management-software-development/#faq-collapse-4)

The cost of developing a custom pharmacy management solution in Singapore depends on factors like features, platform complexity, integration needs, and development timelines. A basic version may start from SGD 20,000, while advanced solutions with custom modules can go significantly higher.
