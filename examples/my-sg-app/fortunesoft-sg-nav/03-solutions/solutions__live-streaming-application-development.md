## Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We are here to help.

[1\. What are the key features of a live streaming application?](https://www.fortunesoftit.com/sg/solutions/live-streaming-application-development/#faq-collapse-0)

Key features include real-time video broadcasting, chat tools, adaptive video quality, content moderation, and analytics. These features ensure an engaging and smooth streaming experience for both viewers and creators.

[2\. How secure is a live streaming platform?](https://www.fortunesoftit.com/sg/solutions/live-streaming-application-development/#faq-collapse-1)

A live streaming platform is secure when using encrypted data transmission, secure login protocols, access control, and real-time monitoring to ensure privacy and safety for users.

[3\. Can the live streaming app be customized to match our business needs?](https://www.fortunesoftit.com/sg/solutions/live-streaming-application-development/#faq-collapse-2)

Yes, Fortunesoft develops customized live streaming apps to align with your business goals. Customization includes branding, functionality, and integration with third-party services to enhance the overall user experience.

[4\. What technologies do you use for live streaming app development?](https://www.fortunesoftit.com/sg/solutions/live-streaming-application-development/#faq-collapse-3)

Technologies like WebRTC, Wowza Streaming Engine, and RTMP (Real-Time Messaging Protocol) are employed to ensure low-latency, high-quality video streaming for a seamless user experience.

[5\. How do you ensure high-quality video streaming with minimal latency?](https://www.fortunesoftit.com/sg/solutions/live-streaming-application-development/#faq-collapse-4)

We use adaptive bitrate streaming, Content Delivery Networks (CDNs), and real-time protocols like WebRTC and RTMP to reduce latency and provide high-quality video playback on any device or network.
