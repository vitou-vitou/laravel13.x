We follow global compliance standards, implementing data encryption, secure payment processing, and user consent mechanisms to ensure adherence to GDPR, PCI DSS, and other regulations.

We use secure APIs, Open Banking protocols, and real-time processing technologies to enable seamless integration with financial institutions and ensure seamless transactions.

[10\. How does Fortunesoft optimize wallets for high transaction volumes?](https://www.fortunesoftit.com/sg/solutions/digital-wallet-development/#faq-collapse-9)
