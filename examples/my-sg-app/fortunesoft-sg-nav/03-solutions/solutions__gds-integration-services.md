## Accord your customers with a wide array of highly efficient and interactive booking solution with our best-in-class GDS software development services

GDS integration provides highly efficient and interactive booking solutions. GDS integration offers an opportunity for the OTAs to expand their booking facilities through contents on air and non-airline for a global reach. When customers get to access a wide range of real-time contents about flight status, prices, hotel availability, and make reservations instantly, the overhead gets reduced. This provides an optimal experience to your customers.

At Fortunesoft, we offer end-to-end GDS integration and online travel agency solutions for various business models like B2B, B2C, B2G, etc. Our developers deploy the industry leading GDS development and integration services that would allow you to manage your travel business efficiently, reduce cost, and boost revenue. Our GDS software development experts are highly experienced in building web services such as booking engines, custom websites, front-office systems for travel agencies, and corporate self-booking tools.

GDS or Global Distribution System is a network system that enables transactions between travel industry service providers like airlines, hotels, and travel agencies. GDS has real-time links to multiple service providers allowing agencies to operate with up-to-date inventory information.

Fortunesoft Singapore specializes in major GDS integration services. We are experienced in meeting the standard norms with our technical expertise skillfully. We offer a plethora of a wide range of services apart from flight booking services. We offer a wide range of services in GDS software development to our Clients in Singapore, Malaysia, Hong kong and Thailand.
