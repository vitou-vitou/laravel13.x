## **What is WordPress?**

In the IT industry, WordPress is a well-known open-source content management system (CMS). It serves as the foundation for millions of corporate websites, e-commerce websites, healthcare websites, blog websites, and many more throughout the world.

WordPress first came out in 2003 and quickly soared to fame as one of the top open-source platforms for developing and managing various sorts of websites and content. At the moment, WordPress powers more than 43% of all online websites.

WordPress is simple to use, and you can use it to set up your ideal company website concerning your content management requirements. WordPress plugins and themes enable startups and companies to manage content, website design, and integrated functions. It has gained immense prominence due to its exceptional back-end and front-end functions.

This CMS platform holds several components that make the website visually pleasing and stand out. Further, it is built using PHP and maintains website data in a MySQL database. Since it is open-source, it could be freely self-hosted. That’s why there is always a great demand for the best WordPress development services in Singapore.
