# Cross-Platform Ops Partner — Pilot Offer

**For:** Individual creators with an active TikTok presence (~10K–500K followers)  
**You create on TikTok. I republish elsewhere — with your approval.**

---

## What I do

- Republish your existing TikTok videos to **YouTube Shorts** and **Instagram Reels**
- Write platform-native **titles, descriptions, tags, and post timing** (not copy-paste)
- Run a **weekly batch** so you stay focused on TikTok
- Send **weekly performance snapshots** and a **monthly revenue statement**

I do **not** create new videos, manage your TikTok account, or negotiate brand deals (unless we agree separately).

---

## What you keep

- **Copyright** on your content
- **Ownership** of your TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram accounts
- **Control** — you approve metadata before anything goes live (pilot default)
- **TikTok revenue** — commission applies only to income from cross-posted Shorts/Reels I publish elsewhere

---

## What you do

- Keep posting on TikTok as usual
- Grant **manager/collaborator access** (not your passwords) to YouTube and Meta

- Provide **clean video files** when possible (no TikTok watermark for YouTube quality)
- Confirm you have rights to cross-publish your content

## Music & watermark note

TikTok sounds often **do not** clear YouTube Content ID. At onboarding you choose:

1. **Skip** problematic videos for YouTube  
2. **Replace/mute audio** (I edit; you’re notified)  
3. **You provide** a clean export without blocked sounds  

For best YouTube quality, share **original files** (Drive/Telegram) instead of watermarked TikTok downloads.
