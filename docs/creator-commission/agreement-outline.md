## 2. Services (in scope)

Operator agrees to:

- [ ] Identify eligible public TikTok videos per agreed scope (new-only vs backlog)
- [ ] Repackage metadata for listed **destination platforms**
- [ ] Schedule and publish after Creator approval (per tier)
- [ ] Monitor basic performance metrics
- [ ] Deliver weekly snapshots and monthly settlement statements

**Explicitly out of scope** (unless separate written fee):

- [ ] Original filming or scripting
- [ ] Heavy video editing beyond compliance trims / audio swap
- [ ] Comment, DM, or community management
- [ ] Brand deal negotiation or sponsorship sales
- [ ] Management of Creator’s primary TikTok posting calendar
- [ ] Facebook Reels (unless listed in Schedule A)

---

## 3. Creator warranties

Creator represents and warrants:

- [ ] Creator owns or controls the submitted Content and has authority to enter this agreement
- [ ] Content does not infringe third-party copyright, trademark, privacy, or publicity rights
- [ ] Content complies with applicable platform terms at time of submission
- [ ] Creator will not submit third-party content without rights
- [ ] Creator will notify Operator of any takedown claim within `[24]` hours

---

## 4. License grant to Operator

- [ ] **Non-exclusive** license (unless premium tier states otherwise)
- [ ] **Worldwide**
- [ ] Purpose: reformat, optimize metadata, publish, and promote on **Schedule A platforms** only
- [ ] Term: agreement duration; **survival** for existing published copies per termination section
- [ ] No transfer of copyright to Operator

---

## 5. Account access

- [ ] Creator retains ownership of all social accounts
- [ ] Operator receives **manager/collaborator/editor** roles only — **no password sharing**
- [ ] List platforms and account identifiers in Schedule A
- [ ] Operator will not transfer access to third parties without consent
- [ ] Creator will not revoke access during active batch processing except for cause

---

## 6. Asset delivery

- [ ] **Originals-first:** Creator provides clean files via `[Drive/Telegram/shared folder]` when available
- [ ] Operator may pull from TikTok for **metadata and backup only** with Creator authorization
- [ ] Watermarked TikTok rips are **not** primary masters for YouTube unless Creator approves in writing
- [ ] File naming and delivery cadence: `[e.g. weekly by Sunday]` 

---

## 7. Music / Content ID policy

Creator selects one default policy at onboarding (Schedule B):

- [ ] **Skip** — do not publish to YouTube if music likely to trigger Content ID
- [ ] **Replace/mute** — Operator may edit audio for compliance with prior notice
- [ ] **Creator export** — Creator supplies clean export without blocked library sounds

- [ ] Operator notifies Creator of strikes or claims within `[24]` hours
- [ ] Creator responsible for underlying music rights unless Operator supplied licensed replacement

---

## 8. Revenue definition

### In scope (commission applies)

- [ ] YouTube AdSense attributable to **Shorts video IDs** Operator published (listed in monthly statement)
- [ ] Instagram / Facebook **program bonuses** tied to Reels Operator published, when platform reporting allows

### Out of scope (default — no commission)

- [ ] TikTok Creator Rewards / Pulse on Creator’s TikTok
- [ ] Brand deals, sponsorships, merch, affiliate unless **Schedule C** addendum
- [ ] Tips, Super Thanks, gifts (unless Schedule C)

**Net revenue** = platform payout after platform fees, not gross views or estimated RPM alone.

---

## 9. Attribution formula

When YouTube provides channel-level AdSense only:

```
attributed_revenue = channel_adsense_payout × (S / T)

S = views in period on Shorts video IDs Operator published
T = total Shorts views on channel in same period
```

- [ ] Same allocation principle documented for Meta if only aggregate insights exist
- [ ] Each statement shows **S, T, payout, formula, result**
- [ ] Rows labeled **Confirmed** (payout received) or **Estimated** (dashboard only)
